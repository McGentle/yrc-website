    About the Youth Resource Center

    The Youth Resorce Centre is an integrated one-stop Centre that provides a wide range of social, health, education, employment and life skills services to youths of the community. it is established to provides empowerment through the sharing of information and knowlege.

    the Centre provides a walk-in youth friendly environment to allow youths access a range of refferal and information services for free as well as a safe and encoraging environment for our youth to explore their full potentials.

    The Centre caters for a broad range of young people ages of 4 - 28, equipping them with the creativity, energy and leadership capacity our community needs to grow and thrive.

    The Centre was incoporated as a Non-Profit Organization in October, 2013 and opened to the public on the 6th of February, 2014.
